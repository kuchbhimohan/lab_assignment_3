Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

List of Ingredients

  • 8 slices Bread
  • 8 slices Cheese
  • Â¼ cup butter
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 8 slices Tomato

Instructions

  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, and set aside
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Spread butter onto one side of each slice of bread
  4. Lay 4 slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
  5. Top with a slice of cheese, 2 slices tomato, bacon, and another slice of cheese.
  6. Cover with a slice of bread, butter side out. Fry sandwiches until golden on both sides.
